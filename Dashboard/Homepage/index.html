    About Us

    KEDAI AYAMAS is the first established store in Malaysia to introduce the sales of HALAL fresh chicken and other chicken based products with modern, hygiene and convenience ambience condition. In tandem with it’s tag line “More than just Chicken”, KEDAI AYAMAS also offers customers ready-to-eat food products such as Nasi Briyani Bukhara Ayam, Nasi Pilaf Ayam Mentega, Nasi Kandar Ayam Masala, Nasi Bijan Ayam Bulgogi, Nasi Kari Hijau Ayam Thai and ready-to-cook products such as its wide range of pastes, spices, herbs and other kitchen-shelf products from special sauces to confectionery and beverages. Currently there are 58 KEDAI AYAMAS stores nationwide comprise of 32 stores owned by KARA and 26 stores by the licensee under KEDAI AYAMAS Licensing Programme. Apart from KEDAI AYAMAS, KARA also involved in the wholesales business activity supplying HALAL fresh chicken and frozen products to distributor and other F&B related establishments.
